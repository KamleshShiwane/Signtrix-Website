About

Signtrix

Be Seen. Be Remembered.

Signtrix solutions is an all in one, new, innovative and captivating advertising package designed to not only get your message across but have it retained in your target demographics' heads.

Launched in 2013 as an operation under jTd Properties Inc, Signtrix combines the necessity of ordering food from a T.V menu with the delights of entertaining patrons through subconscious messages.

jTd Properties Inc was founded by two University of Waterloo students and incorporated in October 2012. The company is located in Waterloo, Ontario. We have launched Signtrix at numerous partner locations across the Greater Toronto Area and are growing fast.​
